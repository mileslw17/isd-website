About ISD

Football brings
people together.
So do we.

ISD was built for international students in the Netherlands. We know what it feels like to arrive in a new city — a new language, new routines, searching for your place.

On the pitch, those barriers disappear. We train together, win together, lose together. Then grab a drink somewhere in Amsterdam.

"You don't have to be the best player. You just have to show up."

— Founder of ISD

What we build

Three things
we stand for.

01

Confidence

Train in an environment where mistakes are part of growth. Every session builds belief — on and off the pitch.

02

Connection

Over 10 nationalities on one pitch. Football is the language everyone speaks — friendships form fast here.

03

Resilience

International student life is hard. This is where you build the mental strength to handle all of it.
